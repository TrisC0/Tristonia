NanoTask AI — Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how NanoTask AI by Tristonia (“we”, “our”, or “us”) handles your information when you use our mobile application.

Last Updated: 16 May 2026

NanoTask AI processes all data entirely on your device. No personal data, tasks, or AI-generated content is transmitted to external servers.

Generative AI Disclosure: NanoTask AI uses generative artificial intelligence to produce AI-generated task breakdowns from user-provided prompts. All AI processing occurs on-device. Users can report offensive or unsatisfactory AI-generated content via dev@tristonia.com.

1. About NanoTask AI

NanoTask AI is a productivity application that uses artificial intelligence to break down tasks into smaller, manageable steps. The app is powered by an on-device AI engine that runs locally on your phone, meaning your tasks and results are processed entirely on your device without being sent to any external server.

1.1 AI Engine Download

To use NanoTask AI’s features, you must download the AI engine. This is a one-time download that enables on-device processing. The download requires an internet connection; however, once the engine is installed, NanoTask AI operates fully offline.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 User-Generated Content (Prompts)

When you use NanoTask AI, you enter task descriptions (“prompts”) that are sent to the on-device AI engine for processing. This constitutes user-generated content. Specifically:

  • What data is sent to the model: The task descriptions (prompts) you type into the app are passed to the AI engine running on your device.
  • Where it is processed: All prompts are processed locally on your device only. No prompt data is transmitted to any external server, cloud service, or third party.
  • How long it is retained: Prompts and AI-generated results are stored locally on your device for as long as the app is installed or until you clear the app’s data. We do not retain any copies of your prompts or results.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

We may collect limited, non-personal technical information to ensure the app functions correctly, including:

  • Device model and operating system version (for compatibility purposes)
  • App crash reports and performance diagnostics
  • General usage analytics (e.g., feature usage frequency, session duration)

This information is collected in aggregate form and cannot be used to identify you personally.

2.3 Information We Do Not Collect

We want to be clear about what we do not collect:

  • The tasks you enter into the app
  • The AI-generated results or task breakdowns
  • Your location data
  • Your contacts, messages, photos, or other personal files
  • Any content processed by the on-device AI engine

3. How We Use Information

The limited technical information we collect is used solely to:

  • Maintain and improve the app’s performance and stability
  • Diagnose technical issues and fix bugs
  • Understand general usage patterns to improve features

We do not use any information for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. The only circumstances under which we might share data are:

  • If required by law or legal process
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Tristonia, our users, or the public

5. AI Content Safety and Responsible Use

5.1 Content Filtering

NanoTask AI includes content safety measures to filter prompts and results that may involve harmful, illegal, or inappropriate content. These filters operate entirely on-device.

5.2 AI Limitations

The on-device AI engine may occasionally produce results that are inaccurate, incomplete, or inappropriate. AI-generated task breakdowns should be reviewed by the user before being acted upon. NanoTask AI is a productivity aid, not a substitute for professional advice.

5.3 Prohibited Uses

You agree not to use NanoTask AI to:

  • Generate content that promotes violence, hatred, or illegal activities
  • Attempt to circumvent the app’s content safety filters
  • Use the app for any purpose that violates applicable laws or regulations

5.4 Reporting Unsatisfactory or Harmful Results

If the AI produces results that you believe are inaccurate, harmful, offensive, or otherwise unsatisfactory, we encourage you to report them. Your reports help us improve our content safety measures and provide a better experience for all users.

To submit a report, please email us at dev@tristonia.com with the following information:

  • A description of the task you entered
  • A description or screenshot of the result produced
  • Why you believe the result is unsatisfactory or harmful
  • Your device model and app version (if known)

We review all reports and use them to improve our safety filters and overall app quality.

6. Data Security

Because NanoTask AI processes data locally on your device, the security of your task data is inherently tied to the security of your device. We recommend:

  • Keeping your device’s operating system up to date
  • Using a secure lock screen on your device
  • Only downloading the app from the official Google Play Store

For the limited technical data we do collect (crash reports and usage analytics), we implement appropriate security measures to protect this information during transmission and storage.

7. Children’s Privacy

NanoTask AI is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at dev@tristonia.com. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Because NanoTask AI processes your tasks locally and does not transmit them to our servers, you maintain full control over your data at all times. You can:

  • Delete your data: Clear the app’s data through your device settings or uninstall the app to remove all locally stored information
  • Use the app offline: After the initial AI engine download, you can use NanoTask AI without any internet connection
  • Opt out of analytics: You may disable analytics data collection in the app’s settings

Depending on your location, you may also have additional rights under applicable data protection laws (such as GDPR or CCPA), including the right to access, correct, or delete any personal information we hold. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.

9. Third-Party Services

NanoTask AI may integrate with the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Services: For app distribution, updates, and basic device compatibility
  • Analytics providers: For aggregated, non-personal usage statistics

These third-party services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them. We do not share your task data or AI-generated results with any third party.

10. Data Retention

Task data and AI-generated results are stored locally on your device and are retained only for as long as the app is installed or until you clear the app’s data. We do not retain copies of your tasks or results on any server.

Aggregated technical data (crash reports and analytics) is retained for a reasonable period to allow us to analyse trends and improve the app, after which it is deleted.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the updated policy on this page and updating the “Last Updated” date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of NanoTask AI after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
