Decision Spinner

Android App

Can't make up your mind? Decision Spinner is a fun and simple way to settle any choice. Just add your options, spin the wheel, and let fate decide. Whether you're picking where to eat, what to watch, or settling a friendly debate — Decision Spinner has you covered.

Features

  • Create custom spinning wheels with your own options
  • Colourful, smooth spin animations
  • Save and reuse your favourite wheels
  • Simple, intuitive interface — no clutter
  • Works offline — no internet required
