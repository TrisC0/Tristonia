Task Root — Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Task Root by Tristonia ("we", "our", or "us") handles your information when you use our mobile application.

Last Updated:

Task Root processes all data entirely on your device. No personal data, tasks, or AI-generated content is transmitted to external servers.

1. About Task Root

Task Root is a productivity application that uses artificial intelligence to break down tasks into smaller, manageable steps. The app is powered by Gemma 4, an open-weight AI model developed by Google and released under the Apache 2.0 licence. The AI engine runs locally on your device, meaning your tasks and results are processed entirely on your phone without being sent to any external server.

1.1 AI Engine Download

To use Task Root's AI features, you must download the Gemma 4 AI engine. This is a one-time download that enables on-device processing. The download requires an internet connection; however, once the engine is installed, Task Root operates fully offline.

1.2 Open-Source Attribution

Gemma 4 is developed by Google and distributed under the Apache 2.0 licence. Task Root is not developed by, affiliated with, or endorsed by Google. Our use of Gemma 4 is in accordance with the terms of the Apache 2.0 licence. For more information about Gemma, visit Google's Gemma documentation.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Data You Provide

When you use Task Root, you enter task descriptions that you would like the AI to break down. This data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

We may collect limited, non-personal technical information to ensure the app functions correctly, including:

  • Device model and operating system version (for compatibility purposes)
  • App crash reports and performance diagnostics
  • General usage analytics (e.g., feature usage frequency, session duration)

This information is collected in aggregate form and cannot be used to identify you personally.

2.3 Information We Do Not Collect

We want to be clear about what we do not collect:

  • The tasks you enter into the app
  • The AI-generated results or task breakdowns
  • Your location data
  • Your contacts, messages, photos, or other personal files
  • Any content processed by the on-device AI engine

3. How We Use Your Information

The limited technical information we collect is used solely to:

  • Maintain and improve the app's performance and stability
  • Diagnose and fix technical issues
  • Understand general usage patterns to improve the user experience

We do not sell, share, or monetise your personal information.

4. On-Device AI Processing

Task Root's core functionality relies on the Gemma 4 AI engine, which runs entirely on your device. This means:

  • Your tasks stay on your device. Task descriptions and AI-generated results are never uploaded to any server.
  • No cloud processing. The AI model runs locally using your device's hardware. No internet connection is required after the initial AI engine download.
  • Your data is under your control. All task data is stored locally on your device and can be deleted at any time by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the app.

5. AI Content Safety and Responsible Use

5.1 Content Safety Filters

Task Root includes built-in content safety measures designed to prevent the AI engine from generating harmful, dangerous, or inappropriate content. These filters are intended to block results that include:

  • Content that promotes, encourages, or provides instructions for self-harm or suicide
  • Content relating to illegal activities, including but not limited to violence, exploitation, drug manufacturing, or weapons production
  • Content that facilitates harassment, abuse, or discrimination
  • Content that could be used for deception, fraud, or manipulation
  • Content involving the exploitation of minors in any form
  • Content that promotes terrorism or extremism

5.2 Limitations of AI Safety Filters

Important: While we take every reasonable measure to prevent harmful content, no AI content filter is perfect. AI-generated results may occasionally be inaccurate, incomplete, or inappropriate. Users should exercise their own judgement when using AI-generated task breakdowns and should not rely on them for safety-critical, medical, legal, or financial decisions.

5.3 Prohibited Uses

You agree not to use Task Root to attempt to generate content that:

  • Promotes self-harm, suicide, or violence against any person or group
  • Facilitates illegal activities or provides instructions for unlawful acts
  • Generates content involving the exploitation or abuse of minors
  • Attempts to circumvent or disable the app's safety filters
  • Infringes upon the rights of any individual or entity
  • Could be used to develop weapons or other instruments of harm

5.4 Reporting Unsatisfactory or Harmful Results

If the AI produces results that you believe are inaccurate, harmful, offensive, or otherwise unsatisfactory, we encourage you to report them. Your reports help us improve our content safety measures and provide a better experience for all users.

To submit a report, please email us at dev@tristonia.com with the following information:

  • A description of the task you entered
  • A description or screenshot of the result produced
  • Why you believe the result is unsatisfactory or harmful
  • Your device model and app version (if known)

We review all reports and use them to improve our safety filters and overall app quality.

6. Data Security

Because Task Root processes data locally on your device, the security of your task data is inherently tied to the security of your device. We recommend:

  • Keeping your device's operating system up to date
  • Using a secure lock screen on your device
  • Only downloading the app from the official Google Play Store

For the limited technical data we do collect (crash reports and usage analytics), we implement appropriate security measures to protect this information during transmission and storage.

7. Children's Privacy

Task Root is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at dev@tristonia.com. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Because Task Root processes your tasks locally and does not transmit them to our servers, you maintain full control over your data at all times. You can:

  • Delete your data: Clear the app's data through your device settings or uninstall the app to remove all locally stored information
  • Use the app offline: After the initial AI engine download, you can use Task Root without any internet connection
  • Opt out of analytics: You may disable analytics data collection in the app's settings

Depending on your location, you may also have additional rights under applicable data protection laws (such as GDPR or CCPA), including the right to access, correct, or delete any personal information we hold. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.

9. Third-Party Services

Task Root may integrate with the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Services: For app distribution, updates, and basic device compatibility
  • Analytics providers: For aggregated, non-personal usage statistics

These third-party services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them. We do not share your task data or AI-generated results with any third party.

10. Data Retention

Task data and AI-generated results are stored locally on your device and are retained only for as long as the app is installed or until you clear the app's data. We do not retain copies of your tasks or results on any server.

Aggregated technical data (crash reports and analytics) is retained for a reasonable period to allow us to analyse trends and improve the app, after which it is deleted.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the updated policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of Task Root after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

12. Permissions and Device Data

Task Root may request the following permissions to provide its functionality:

  • Storage: To save the AI engine and app data locally on your device
  • Network Access: Required only for the initial AI engine download and optional analytics

We do not access your contacts, messages, photos, camera, microphone, location, or any other sensitive device data.
