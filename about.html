About Me

The person behind Tristonia and the apps that come from it.

I'm an independent Android developer who uses imagination and creativity to build apps that are actually useful.

How It Started

Tristonia started as a creative side project. I wanted to build a fantasy world that people could explore, something with floating islands, castles, waterfalls, and all the things I thought would be fun to bring to life in 3D.

Somewhere along the way, it became more than just a world. It became the home for everything I build. The apps I create, the ideas I experiment with, and the things I put out into the world all live under the Tristonia name.

What I Do

I design and develop Android apps. I like taking ideas that seem complex and turning them into something simple, clean, and easy to use. Every app I build starts with a question: "Would this actually help someone?" If the answer is yes, I build it.

I also enjoy experimenting with on-device AI, interactive 3D, and finding ways to make technology feel less like technology and more like something you just enjoy using.

What Matters to Me

Creativity

I don't build boring apps. If I can find a more imaginative way to solve a problem, that's the route I take.

Simplicity

Good apps don't need a tutorial. I aim for clean design and intuitive experiences every time.

Privacy

Your data is yours. I build apps that respect that, and wherever possible, everything stays on your device.

Quality

I'd rather take longer and get it right than rush something out that doesn't feel finished.
