About Me

Independent Android developer. Imagination is the only limit.

I build Android apps that solve real problems in creative ways. No team, no investors, no committees. Just ideas turned into apps.

Who I Am

I'm an independent Android developer building apps under the name Tristonia. I work alone, which means every app I release is something I personally care about. If it's on the Play Store with my name on it, it's because I believed it was worth building.

How I Work

Every app starts the same way. I notice something that could be done better, or I have an idea that won't leave me alone. From there, I design it, build it, and put it out into the world. I keep things simple, respect your privacy, and focus on making apps that do what they're supposed to do without wasting your time.

Why Tristonia

Tristonia is just my corner of the internet. It's where my apps live, where I share what I'm working on, and where you can find out more about the things I build. Nothing more, nothing less.

What I Care About

Creativity

I don't build boring apps. If there's a more imaginative way to solve a problem, that's the route I take.

Simplicity

Good apps don't need a tutorial. If you have to think about how to use it, I haven't done my job.

Privacy

Your data is yours. I build apps that respect that, and wherever possible, everything stays on your device.

Quality

I'd rather take longer and get it right than rush something out that doesn't feel finished.
