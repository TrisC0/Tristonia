About the studio

An indie studio. Every idea, on purpose.

I build Android apps under Tristonia Ltd: no team, no roadmap meetings, just me picking the ideas that deserve to exist and building them properly.

Who I Am

An indie studio with one standard: worth shipping.

I'm an indie Android developer building apps under Tristonia Ltd. Every app released is something I personally care about. If it's on the Play Store with the Tristonia name on it, I stood behind every screen of it before it shipped.

"I'd rather take longer and get it right than rush something out that doesn't feel finished."
How I Work

Notice the problem. Sit with it. Then build the answer.

Every app starts the same way. I notice something that could be done better, or I have an idea that won't leave me alone. From there, I design it, build it, and put it out into the world.

No dark patterns, no bloated feature lists. Just an app that does the one thing it promised, without wasting your time.

— Tristonia
