Manifesto

Apps that solve real problems in creative ways, not more of the same.

Tristonia Ltd is an indie Android studio with one clear standard: every release has to be worth shipping under the Tristonia name. That means tasteful design, thoughtful interactions, and real respect for whoever's on the other side of the screen.

I keep things simple, I keep things private, and I ship only when the work is worth it.
