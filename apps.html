Decision Spinner

Android App · Royal Edition

The game-show wheel for everyday choices.

Can't decide what to eat? Who's washing up? Where to go next weekend? Decision Spinner is a beautifully crafted spinning wheel that turns indecision into a moment. Tap the wheel, watch it spin, hear the satisfying ticks slow down, and let the winner land where it lands.

Features

  • Six hand-crafted themes — Game Show, Roulette, Alloy Wheel, Carnival, Cartwheel, Minimal
  • Real game-show physics with a flapper peg that ticks every slice
  • Wood-on-wood tick sound and a C-major arpeggio win chime
  • 220-particle confetti burst in the theme's palette
  • Weight options 1× to 5×, tournament mode, history of the last 25 winners
  • No ads, no accounts, no tracking — everything stays on your device

ShrinkRay

Android App

Big tasks feel overwhelming? I built ShrinkRay to use on-device AI to break down any task into smaller, manageable steps. All processing happens entirely on your device, so your data never leaves your phone. No cloud, no limits, no subscriptions. Just describe what you need to do, and ShrinkRay will map out a clear path from start to finish.

Features

  • AI-powered task breakdown that turns big goals into actionable steps
  • Fully on-device processing, no data sent to external servers
  • Unlimited use. Pay once, no subscriptions or token limits
  • Built-in content safety filters for responsible AI use
  • Works offline once the AI engine is downloaded
